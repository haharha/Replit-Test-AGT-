AGT Parenting Assessment App - Complete Build Prompt
Project Overview
Build a professional parenting assessment web application for Absolute Genetic Technologies (AGT) that helps parents identify their child's behavioral and health concerns, then recommends personalized PDF resources based on severity ratings.

Purpose: Rate 14 concerns on 1-5 scale
Instructions:

1 = Not at all  |  2 = Rarely  |  3 = Sometimes  |  4 = Often  |  5 = Very Often

"My child doesn't share much with me, and I often wonder what's going on in his/her mind."

"My child can be low in energy and falls sick easily. I worry about missing nutrients."
"My child is a picky eater, and I want to ensure they get essential nutrients."
